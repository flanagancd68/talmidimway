Revelation 1 Part 1 (1:1-11): Greetings
This third lesson is titled, “Greetings” because John includes an introduction and salutation very much in the style of correspondence of that day. John will give us the sequence of the delivery: From God to Yeshua/Jesus, from Yeshua to an angel, from an angel to John, and from John to Yeshua’s servants.
